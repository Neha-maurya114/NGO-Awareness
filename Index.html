About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating sustainable social impact across India.

The foundation works in education, environmental conservation, women empowerment, animal welfare, humanitarian support and skill development through dedicated volunteers and strategic partnerships.

Our Recognitions

  • ✔ Section 8 Registered
  • ✔ 80G & 12A Certified
  • ✔ CSR-1 Registered
  • ✔ NITI Aayog Registered
  • ✔ ISO 9001:2015 Certified
